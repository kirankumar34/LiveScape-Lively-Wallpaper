# Privacy Policy for LiveScape – Live Wallpaper New Tab

Last updated: March 2026

## 1. Overview

LiveScape – Live Wallpaper New Tab ("the extension") is designed to enhance your browser experience by providing customizable wallpapers and widgets. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

---

## 2. Data Collection

This extension does **NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.

We do not collect:

* Personal information (name, email, etc.)
* Browsing history
* Cookies or tracking data
* Location data

---

## 3. Local Data Storage

All data used by the extension is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's storage APIs.

This may include:

* Selected wallpaper preferences
* Widget settings
* User customization options

This data **never leaves your device**.

---

## 4. Permissions Usage

The extension uses the following permission:

* **storage** → to save user preferences locally

No other permissions are used to access personal or sensitive information.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

This extension does **NOT use any third-party analytics, tracking tools, or external servers**.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **NOT share, sell, or transfer any user data** to third parties.

---

## 7. Security

Since all data is stored locally and no external servers are used, your data remains secure within your browser environment.

---

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
